Key Advertising Metric Formulas
CTR
CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) × 100
11,568 / 2,40,000 × 100 = 4.82%
CPC
CPC = Total Spend / Total Clicks
₹1,43,443 / 11,568 = ₹12.40
CAC
CAC = Total Spend / New Customers
₹1,43,443 / 770 = ₹186
ROAS
ROAS = Revenue / Ad Spend

ROI
ROI = (Revenue − Cost) / Cost × 100
(5,45,000 − 1,43,443) / 1,43,443 = 280%
CVR
CVR = (Conversions / Clicks) × 100
1,408 / 11,568 × 100 = 12.17%
