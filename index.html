Recommendations — Making Zepto More Popular

SEO improvements, campaign optimizations, and website growth strategies

SEO Improvements
Mobile page speed is at 62% — needs to reach 90%+. Use lazy loading, image compression (WebP), and CDN. This alone reduces bounce rate by ~15%.
Target long-tail keywords like "10 minute grocery delivery Bangalore" — lower competition but very high buyer intent and conversion potential.
Add FAQ schema markup on product pages to capture Google rich snippets. This increases SERP real estate and organic CTR by 20–30%.
Build backlinks through guest posts on food blogs, tech review sites, and local business directories to push DA from 72 → 80+.
Campaign Optimizations
Implement retargeting campaigns for cart abandoners (48% checkout drop-off). Estimated 20–30% cart recovery rate using Facebook pixel + Google remarketing.
Use Lookalike Audiences in Facebook Ads based on high-LTV customers to reduce CAC below ₹150 while maintaining quality traffic.
A/B test ad creatives monthly — rotate images, headlines, and CTAs. Ad fatigue reduces CTR by 40% after 2 weeks of same creative.
Increase SendPulse push notification frequency during peak hours: 7–9 AM, 12–2 PM, 6–9 PM. Personalize with product browsing history.
